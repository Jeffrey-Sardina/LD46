Names that begin in a vowel are traditionally female, others traditionally male
Last names are often 'of (name of parent)'
    the parent chosen is the elder of the two, and can change each generation
    names are additive, but in mot cases only that last two or fewer are shown (more is more formal)
    ex a female name: Ifel nith Ottho nith Thláuu nith Exlil (very long, thus also verry formal)
        a male name: ̪Pothwo nith Bdhe (A shorter, colloquial / common name)
