Several expressions are very commonly used among the Ccaizhdi peoples.

Hello (to one)      igo hluf bo
Hello (to many)     igai hluf bo
Thank you (to one)  igo xa dzifst
Thank you (to many) igai xa dzifst
You're welcome      dzifst
Please              dzifst ab
Sorry               ibo xa shezh
Bye                 Ppaeftto shaixae!
