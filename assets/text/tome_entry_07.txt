Noun number
    paucal (only a few):   -(e)f
    plural (many):         -(o)sh
    Only add the vowel if the last letter of the word in a consonant.

Tense suffixes
    present:                  no change
    past:                     (ae)g
    since morning past:       (ae)pp
    future:                   (i)x

The Ccaizhdi do not use articles (a / an or the)
